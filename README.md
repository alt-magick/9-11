# 9-11
A Conspiracy

The pilot episode of a spin off of the popular TV series, 'The X-Files,' called 'The Lone Gunmen.' premiered in March 2001. 

In this episode an airliner is hijacked (via remote control of the plane's autopilot) and the hijackers attempt to crash the plane into the World Trade Center.

This show was aired 6 months before the actual events of 9/11. 

The episode's plot indicates that the hijacking was committed as an act of greed by an American arms manufacturer, to ultimately increase its weapons sales by invoking US retaliation against a scapegoated, anti-American, extremist dictator.
